AM broadcast

AM broadcast stations, with notes especially on DX’ing baseball games.

Generally speaking, baseball games are not broadcast for free on the Internet, so you have to do some sleuthing to find a general purpose radio receiver that can be tuned to the games.

  • CJCL 590 Toronto

    • CJCL 590 Toronto can be heard in Rochester, NY. http://rx.jimlill.com:8073/?f=590.00amz10 It is the radio home of the Toronto Blue Jays.

  • KCSP 610 Kansas City

    • KCSP 610 is the flagship station for the Kansas City Royals.

  • KDAL 610 Duluth MN

    • KDAL Duluth 610 is a member of the Minnesota Twins “Treasure Island” radio baseball network. Tune via Global Tuners: https://www.globaltuners.com/receiver/1858-Duluth-MN-1

  • WTMJ 620 Milwaukee

    • WTMJ is the flagship station of the Milwaukee Brewers radio network. 50000 watts daytime, 10000 watts night time. http://www.wtmj.com/ Listen via KiwiSDR: Farmington Hills: http://misdr.duckdns.org/?f=620.00lsbz10 (LSB to avoid interference from 630 AM CFCO Chatham Ontario).

  • WFAN 660 New York City

    • WFAN Sports Radio 66AM/101.1FM is the radio home of the New York Yankees.

  • WSCR 670 Chicago

    • WSCR 670 “The Score” broadcasts Chicago Cubs games. Listen via KiwiSDR: KH6ILT Elida OH: http://65.29.112.189:8073/?f=670.00amz10

  • KNBR 680 San Francisco

    • KNBR is the flagship station of the San Francisco Giants. It is a Cumulus station.

  • WLW 700 Cincinnati

    • WLW AM 700 is the flagship station of the Cincinnati Reds radio network. Via KiwiSDR: Elida OH: http://65.29.112.189:8073/?f=700.00amz10 Farmington Hills MI: http://misdr.duckdns.org/?f=700.00amz10 Fenton MI: http://dentonhill-sdr.moses.bz/?f=700.00amz10

  • KIRO 710 Seattle

    • KIRO-AM is 710 ESPN Seattle with sports programming. KIRO is the flagship station for the Seattle Mariners.

  • KTKR 760 San Antonio TX

    • KTKR 760 San Antonio broadcasts Houston Astros games. Tune via Global Tuners: https://www.globaltuners.com/receiver/1155/js2

  • KOA 850 Denver

    • KOA 850 is the flagship station of the Colorado Rockies. Kiwisdr: http://foxgulch.net:8073/?f=850.00amz10

  • WLS 890 Chicago Illinois

    • WLS 890 is the flagship station for the Chicago White Sox. Via KiwiSDR Farmington Hills MI: http://misdr.duckdns.org/?f=890.00amz10 Via KiwiSDR Elida OH: http://65.29.112.189:8073/?f=890.00amz10

  • KKNE 940 Honolulu

    • KKNE “AM 940 Hawaii” broadcasts in Hawaiian with traditional Hawaiian music and talk aimed at Hawaiian speakers. KiwiSDR at NH6XO: http://72.235.91.131:8073/?f=940.00amz10

  • WINZ 940 Miami

    • WINZ 940 is the flagship station of the Miami Marlins baseball radio network. “Miami’s Sports Station”. Via KiwiSDR NO2CW: http://qth.ddns.net:8073/?f=940.00amz8 Home page: http://940winz.iheart.com/ Twitter: https://twitter.com/940WINZ

  • WROC 950 Rochester NY

    • http://rx.jimlill.com:8073/?f=950.00amz10 Sports Radio 950, home to coverage of the Buffalo Sabres, Rochester Knighthawks, and Rochester Rattlers.

  • WWJ 950 Detroit

    • WWJ is mostly a news station, with traffic and weather together on the 8s. There’s an occasional sports feed, which varies depending on the year.

    A CBS radio station.

  • KIKI 990 Honolulu

    • Fox Sports 990 - Hawaii’s Home for Fox Sports & the Los Angeles Dodgers. KiwiSDR: http://72.235.91.131:8073/?f=990.00amz10

  • WMVP 1000 Chicago

    • WMVP 1000 is ESPN Radio for Chicago. This station comes in clear at night to the Detroit market.

    • via Farmington Hills, MI: http://misdr.duckdns.org/?f=1000.00amz10
    • via Elida, OH: http://65.29.112.189:8073/?f=1000.00amz10

  • WINS 1010 New York City

    • 1010 WINS has 24 hour news coverage from New York City. A CBS radio station.

  • WTKA 1050 Ann Arbor

    • WTKA 1050 Ann Arbor broadcasts Detroit Tigers baseball.

  • KNX 1070 Los Angeles

    • KNX 1070 is an all-news radio station for Los Angeles, California.

  • WBAL 1090 Baltimore

    • WBAL 1090 Baltimore is the flagship station of the Baltimore Orioles baseball network. KiwiSDR N4TVC: http://n4tvc.zapto.org:8073/?f=1090.00amz10

  • WTAM 1100 Cleveland

    • WTAM 1100 AM is a 50,000 watt Class A radio station and the AM flagship station of the Cleveland Indians baseball and Cleveland Cavaliers basketball radio networks.

  • KMOX 1120 St Louis MO

    • KMOX 1120 St Louis is the flagship station of the St Louis Cardinals baseball team. KMOX broadcasts with 50,000 watts clear channel. KiwiSDR: Central Indiana: http://38.86.67.206:8073/?f=1120.00amz10

  • KQNA 1130 Prescott Valley AZ

    • KQNA 1130 Prescott Valley is part of the Arizona Diamondbacks radio network. http://no1d.duckdns.org:8073/?f=1130.00amz10 http://www.kqna.com/

  • KHTK 1140 Sacramento

    • KHTK serves as flagship station for Sacramento Kings basketball and Sacramento Mountain Lions and UC Davis Aggies football, as well as Sacramento’s radio home for Oakland Athletics baseball, Oakland Raiders football and San Jose Sharks hockey. KiwiSDR: N3LGA Sunnyvale, CA: http://sdr.n3lga.com:8073/?f=1140.00amz10

  • WIMA 1150 Lima Ohio

    • WIMA 1150 Lima Ohio is in the Cincinnati Reds radio network. http://65.29.112.189:8073/?f=1150.00amz10

  • CFGO 1200 Ottawa Ontario

    • CFGO 1200 is “TSN Radio 1200” Ottawa Ontario. Sports coverage: Ottawa Senators (NHL hockey) Ottawa 67’s (OHL hockey) Ottawa Redblacks (CFL football) Ottawa Fury FC (USL soccer) Listen via KiwiSDR. http://www.ma-ku.com:8073/?f=1200.00amz10

  • WCBC 1270 Cumberland MD

    • Local station WCBC-AM Cumberland MD broadcasts Pittsburgh Pirates games. http://n4ldr-sdr.ddns.net:9000/?f=1270.00amz10 https://www.wcbcradio.com/

  • WXYT 1270 Detroit

    • WXYT 1270 AM and WXYT-FM 97.1 are the radio home of the Detroit Tigers.

  • KRKO 1380 Everett WAS

    • KRKO 1380 Everett Washington carries games of the Class A Everett AquaSox baseball team. Via KiwiSDR U Victoria: http://kiwisdr.ece.uvic.ca:8073/?f=1380.00amz10 Via KiwiSDR Sammamish WA: http://24.17.196.180:8073/?f=1380.00amz10

  • KREF 1400 Norman OK

    • KREF 1400 in Norman Oklahoma is an affiliate of the St Louis Cardinals radio network. St. Louis Cardinals (MLB) & St. Louis Rams (NFL) games, Oklahoma University sports. KiwiSDR: W5TSU Norman OK: http://sdr.w5tsu.net:8073/?f=1400.00amz10

  • KSTP 1500 Minneapols

    • KSTP Minneapolis is the flagship station for the Minnesota Twins baseball club.

  • WFED 1500 Washington DC

    • WFED 1500 “Federal News Radio” broadcasts Washington Nationals baseball from Washington, DC. It is a 50,000 watt Class A station.

  • KEBC 1560 Oklahoma City OK

    • KEBC 1560 Oklahoma City is an affiliate of the Texas Rangers radio network. Via W5TWU KiwiSDR: http://sdr.w5tsu.net:8073/?f=1560.00amz10
